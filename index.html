The Orb: Jefferey’s emotional body.

The glowing orb isn’t decoration. It is Jefferey’s visible “body” — a single, living symbol that reflects how your Personal AI Shadow feels and where its attention is.

As Jefferey watches over your digital life, the orb changes shape, colour, and movement to express mood: calm when everything is quiet, alert when tasks pile up, protective when something looks wrong, and bright when things go well.

Over time, the orb becomes a language you instantly understand at a glance — especially for people who don’t want to read dashboards or alerts.

  • Shape shows body language — relaxed, tense, drooping, or explosive.
  • Colour shows emotional state — cool blues for calm, warm golds for happy, deep reds for danger or anger.
  • Motion shows intensity — slow breathing when all is normal, jittery movement when Jefferey is on high alert.
  • In future versions, the orb will be tied directly to real events in your life: bills due, scams detected, systems secure, or loved ones safely checked-in.

The world’s first Personal AI Shadow.

Jefferey AI isn’t just another chatbot. It is a lifelong digital presence that stays with one person, processing locally and protecting your privacy absolutely.

It remembers your routines, understands your preferences, and evolves quietly in the background — just like a shadow.

Built for one person, not the crowd.

One human, one shadow. Jefferey’s entire memory, personality, and behaviour are tuned to you — not to millions of anonymous users.

Why a shadow?

A shadow is constant. It moves when you move. It reflects who you are. Jefferey brings that idea into the digital world: a calm British-butler intelligence that always knows you, always stays local, and always works for you alone.

  • Follows you across devices and environments.
  • Remembers context over days, months, and years.
  • Feels familiar, composed, and quietly present.

Private by design. Powerful by nature.
